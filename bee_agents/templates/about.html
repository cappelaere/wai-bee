The Scholarship Management System is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to streamline and enhance the scholarship application review process. It automates the analysis of scholarship applications, providing consistent, objective evaluations while reducing manual review time by up to 80%.

Built specifically for Women in Aviation International (WAI) scholarships, the system processes applications for multiple scholarship programs including the Valerie Delaney Memorial Scholarship and Evans Wings Scholarship, ensuring fair and thorough evaluation of each applicant.
